Ammu's Homestay, nestled in the scenic Wayanad district of Kerala, offers a peaceful retreat in the heart of nature, surrounded by the forests of Kerala, Karnataka, and Tamil Nadu. Known for its rural charm and rich tribal heritage, Wayanad is home to the Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary, a lush region with diverse wildlife. Our homestay provides comfortable accommodation with three spacious family bedrooms—one on the first floor and two on the ground floor. The first-floor bedroom is equipped with three single cots and an additional folding cot, allowing flexible sleeping arrangements, while the ground-floor rooms feature king-size beds and the option to add extra folding cots. Additionally, our first-floor hall boasts a rooftop pergola and can accommodate multiple cots, making it perfect for group gatherings. The ground floor living room can be converted into a sleeping area using folding mattresses for even more space. Whether you're looking for a relaxing escape or an adventurous stay amidst Wayanad's natural beauty, Ammu's Homestay ensures a comfortable and memorable experience.
