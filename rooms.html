Rooms

Our Rooms & Accommodation Capacity

At Ammu's Homestay, we offer a range of cozy and versatile accommodation options designed to provide comfort and relaxation for families, groups, and solo travelers. Our rooms are thoughtfully arranged to cater to different needs, ensuring that everyone feels at home. Here's a look at our accommodation facilities:

1. Family Bedrooms

First Floor Family Bedroom

This room accommodates up to four guests with three single cots and one folding cot. The single cots can be arranged together to form double or triple beds, or used separately as individual beds, depending on your preference.

Ground Floor Family Bedrooms

Both ground floor rooms are generously sized and feature king-size beds, offering plenty of space for relaxation. One of these rooms can also accommodate two folding cots if extra sleeping arrangements are needed.

2. Spacious First Floor Hall with Rooftop Pergola

Our expansive first-floor hall provides an open and airy space for larger groups or families who prefer a more communal setting. This room features:

  • Two double cots and two single cots
  • Option to add one or two folding cots, if required
  • A charming rooftop pergola, perfect for enjoying the outdoors in a sheltered setting

Please Review Before Booking

We encourage all guests to review the available sleeping arrangements before confirming their booking to ensure that the room setup meets your preferences for space and comfort.

At Ammu's Homestay, we strive to create a warm, welcoming environment where every guest feels at ease, whether you're here for a short stay or a longer retreat.

Thank you, and we look forward to hosting you!
