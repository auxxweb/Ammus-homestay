Mission & Vision

Welcome to Ammu's Homestay. At Ammu's Homestay, we invite you to experience the true essence of Kerala's hospitality amidst the serene beauty of Wayanad. Nestled in the heart of mist-covered peaks and verdant landscapes, our "Gold" classified homestay offers a warm and cozy retreat, where comfort meets nature. Whether you’re looking for a peaceful escape or an adventure into the hills, Ammu's Homestay provides the perfect setting to relax and unwind. Let us make your stay a memorable one, where you’ll always feel at home.

At Ammu's Homestay, enjoy the warmth of Kerala's hospitality in the peaceful beauty of Wayanad. Our "Gold" classified homestay offers a cozy and comfortable stay, surrounded by misty peaks and lush greenery. Whether you're here to relax or explore, you'll feel right at home. Ammu's Homestay, nestled in the scenic Wayanad district of Kerala, offers a peaceful retreat in the heart of nature, surrounded by the forests of Kerala, Karnataka, and Tamil Nadu. Known for its rural charm and rich tribal heritage, Wayanad is home to the Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary, a lush region with diverse wildlife. Our homestay provides comfortable accommodation with three spacious family bedrooms—one on the first floor and two on the ground floor. The first-floor bedroom is equipped with three single cots and an additional folding cot, allowing flexible sleeping arrangements, while the ground-floor rooms feature king-size beds and the option to add extra folding cots. Additionally, our first-floor hall boasts a rooftop pergola and can accommodate multiple cots, making it perfect for group gatherings. The ground floor living room can be converted into a sleeping area using folding mattresses for even more space. Whether you're looking for a relaxing escape or an adventurous stay amidst Wayanad's natural beauty, Ammu's Homestay ensures a comfortable and memorable experience.
